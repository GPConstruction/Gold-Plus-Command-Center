📋 BCA & BizSafe Licence Renewal
– 0 ▸
🔴
Loading…
An expired BCA CRS licence means you cannot legally tender for or execute construction contracts. BizSafe lapse removes WSH compliance status. Both must be renewed before expiry — do not wait for reminders.
⛑️ Work Injury & Public Liability Insurance
– 0 ▸
🔴
Loading…
Work Injury Compensation (WIC) insurance is mandatory under the Work Injury Compensation Act for all workers. Public Liability covers third-party claims on site. Both must remain valid at all times — a lapse means your workers are unprotected and you cannot operate legally on any site.
🎓 Coretrade — Workers Without Certification
– 0 ▸
💰
Loading…
Each worker without Coretrade costs $900/month levy instead of $500 — a $400/month penalty per worker. Getting workers certified as soon as they're eligible is the highest-return admin task available. BCA Coretrade requires 2 years work experience in the trade. Register via BCA's BCSS portal.
